 REVIEWS

  Verdict

   With the K8 Note, Lenovo has tried hard to satisfy those who need a bit
   of everything ranging from a decent display, impressive processor, great
   camera performance and a considerable battery life. The smartphone could
   be a go-to device for those who want a multimedia device in at a budget
   price tag. The 3GB RAM+32GB version is priced at Rs 12,999,while the
   4GB RAM+64GB storage version is priced at Rs 13,999.
   The Lenovo K8 Note surpasses the K6 Note in almost every possible sphere.
