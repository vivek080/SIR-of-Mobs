 REVIEWS

  Verdict

   The Nokia 8 offers a lot of great features such as a powerful chipset, dual-sensor
   camera and a perfectly sized QHD display, but compared to a lot of the competition
   right now it struggles to offer anything unique. With a super-high price point,
   the Nokia 8 is a good phone but far from a great one.

   For
   Beautiful design, especially with shiny effect
   Dual-sensor rear camera with interesting software
   Gorgeous QHD display

   Against
   No hugely unique features
   It's not waterproof
   Price is a touch higher than we'd hoped.
