  Verdict

   There’s no contest: the LG V20 is the most feature-packed smartphone of
   the year. It’s a phablet that mostly nails the basics and adds on a ton of
   niche features, some of which you might not use all that much.

   For
   Removable battery
   Wide-angle images never get old
   Audiophile-grade hardware

   Against
   Varying utility of second screen
   Just missed out on the Snapdragon 821
   Disappointing camera performance
