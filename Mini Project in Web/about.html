   WHAT ARE WE ?

  Sir of Mobs is the largest publication dedicated to the world's most widely used mobile review website.
  Every month, we influence an audience of 1 lakh professionals in the tech industry, technology enthusiasts,
  and savvy millennials. Our team is a diverse coalition of expert bloggers, device reviewers, videographers,
  and Mobile developers who have come together under a united passion: a love of mobile technology and
  the determination to deliver top-notch content.


   OUR HISTORY

  At Sir of Mobs, we've had our eyes locked to the mobile landscape ever since 2016. From our humble beginnings as a microscopic
  but passionate team, we've grown into one of the most respected voices in tech reporting. Our commitment to quality and editorial
  integrity make us the go-to resource for Android fans the world over, but the site hasn't always looked like it currently does.
