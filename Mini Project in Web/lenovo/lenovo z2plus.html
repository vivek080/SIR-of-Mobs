 REVIEWS

  Verdict

   The Lenovo Z2 Plus is the best smartphone to buy in the sub-20K smartphone category.
   The Qualcomm Snapdragon 820 is undoubtedly one of the most powerful mobile
   processors out there today. This SoC had already shown its prowess in the LG G5,
   HTC 10, OnePlus 3, Xiaomi Mi 5, LeEco Le Max 2, and basically every flagship phone
   available today. However, no one expected it on a sub-20k smartphone, but here we are
   with the Lenovo Z2 Plus.
