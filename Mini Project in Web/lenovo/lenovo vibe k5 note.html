 REVIEWS

  Verdict

   A beautiful metal unibody construction, impressive performance, and excellent
   battery life highlight the solid smartphone experience that is available with the
   Lenovo Vibe K5 Note. The camera is a bit of a let down, but that is somewhat
   expected when considering its price point.

   Positives
   Solid metal unibody design
   Good performance and impressive gaming experience
   Great battery life
   Clean software package, with useful extras

   Negatives
   Lackluster camera
   Audio a let down compared to predecessor (Vibe K4 Note)
